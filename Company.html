Overall Company Goals and Objectives:

-To bring warm climate food growth out of the south and into regions that are normally thought of as seasonal only.

-To create multiple food systems that feed people in many different situations according to location.
Whether you are in the country, the city, an old warehouse, or remotely located these systems provide food with very minimal:

  • Costs from grid inputs like gas and electricity
  • Costs to dispose of wastes that can not be recycled
  • Cost loss due to deficencies during plant growth

These systems can work together to provide comprehensive food growth of many varieties within many regions internationally.
