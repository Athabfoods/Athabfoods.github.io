Overall Company Goals and Objectives:

  1. To bring warm climate food growth out of the south and into regions that are normally thought of as seasonal only.


  2. To create multiple food production systems that feed people in many different situations and locations.

  3. Incorporate designs that are feasible in the country, the city, an old warehouse, remotely located, in a highrise, a basement business, a rooftop, somewhere really cold, or somewhere that has more dark than light for part of a season.

    4. We plan to accomplish this by reducing or removing:

    • Costs from grid inputs like gas and electricity

    • Costs to dispose of wastes that can not be recycled

    • Cost loss due to deficencies during plant growth

    • Costs of feedstock materials to start a processes

    • Costs of water

    • Costs of fertilizers, herbicides, pesticides, and the likes

    • Among others

  4. Our goal is to show how easy it is to grow plants certain ways.
